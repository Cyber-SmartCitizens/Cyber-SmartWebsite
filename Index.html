Our Mission.

At Cyber-Smart Citizens, we strive to teach everyone how to be safe on the internet, and to easily combat the many scams that make them vulnerable. Since our online world is constantly changing, we update our techniques daily to combat anything new that may persist.

About Us →

Teach.

We teach and uncover the most specific details to ensure complete knowlegde on the topic of internet fraud.

Protect.

We strive to protect our communites and the people within them from the devastating effects of internet fraud.

Inspire.

We hope to spread our knowlegde on the issue of internet fraud to truly shift our internet into a safe enviroment.

The Problem.

Internet fraud is constantly becoming more advanced, impacting millions with losses in the billions. New scams and schemes seem to appear from every corner of the internet.

Fact.

In 2021, Gen Xers, Millennials, and Gen Z young adults (ages 18-59) were 34% more likely than older adults (ages 60 and over) to report losing money to fraud. The median individual reported fraud loss by people 18-59 was $500 in 2021.

- The Federal Trade Commission (December 8th, 2023)
